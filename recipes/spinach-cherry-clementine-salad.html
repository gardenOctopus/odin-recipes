Spinach Cherry Clementine Salad

Description:

I started making this recipe in college. At the time, I thought that adding berries or fruit to a lettuce salad was a novel idea, but now I know that this is very common.

This is a pretty versatile recipe. The spinach can be replaced with a spring salad mix or arugula, and berries can be added to it as well.

Ingredients

  • Spinach (4 cups)
  • Pitted Cherries (1/2 cup)
  • Clementine (2)
  • Walnuts (2 tbsp)

Steps

  1. Pit cherries and peel oranges
  2. Place ingredients in a bowl
  3. Add dressing of your choice
