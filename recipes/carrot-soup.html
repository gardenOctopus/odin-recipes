Carrot Soup

Description:

I’ve been making this carrot soup recipe since I was in college, so it’s a nostalgic recipe for me. It’s a great recipe for autumn, winter, or when you’re trying to use us the remaining carrots in your fridge.

It’s a fairly simple recipe to follow, as it only has four steps. It is also budget-friendly if you already have the spices on hand. The potato is optional, you can make this recipe without it.

Ingredients

  • Chopped Onion (1)
  • Garlic Cloves (2)
  • Ginger (1 tbsp)
  • Cumin (1 tsp)
  • Paprika (1 tsp)
  • Cayenne
  • Chopped Carrot (2 cups)
  • Potato (1)
  • Lemon Juice (1 tbsp)
  • Honey (1 tbsp)
  • Broth (4 cups)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in pan. Add onion, garlic, and ginger. Saute for 5 minutes
  2. Add spices
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients. Boil and cook for 25 minutes
  4. Cool and puree
